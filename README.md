# Childcare-Pricing-Trends
Overview
This project analyzes childcare pricing trends across the United States to highlight the growing financial burden placed on families. Through a combination of data analysis, visualization, and storytelling, the project presents a compelling narrative on the rising cost of childcare and its broader socioeconomic implications.
The work is designed not only as an analytical exercise, but also as a policy-oriented communication tool aimed at supporting discussions around government intervention and childcare reform.
________________________________________
Objectives
•	Analyze national and regional trends in childcare costs 
•	Quantify the financial burden on families over time 
•	Identify geographic disparities in affordability 
•	Communicate insights through data storytelling and visualization 
•	Support policy discussions around childcare reform
